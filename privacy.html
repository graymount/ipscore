Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 26, 2024

1. Information Collection

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect the following technical information:

  • IP Address Information: Your public IP address, used for security analysis and geolocation identification
  • Browser Information: User-Agent, browser type, version and language settings
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Network Information: ISP, time zone, DNS server information
  • Access Logs: Access time, page path, referrer page

1.2 Actively Provided Information

Information you may actively provide to us includes:

  • Information submitted through contact forms
  • IP addresses manually entered for detection
  • Feedback and suggestion content

2. Information Use

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: Perform IP security analysis, threat detection and scoring
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns, optimize website performance and user experience
  • Security Protection: Detect and prevent malicious activities and abuse
  • Statistical Analysis: Generate anonymous usage statistics
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Share with trusted third-party service providers, limited to what is necessary to provide services
  • Legal Requirements: As required by laws, regulations or court orders
  • Security Protection: To protect our rights, property or safety, as well as the safety of users and the public
  • Anonymous Data: Share statistical data in anonymous, aggregated form

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

4.1 Cookie Usage

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your language preference settings
  • Analyze website usage
  • Provide personalized experiences
  • Serve relevant advertisements

4.2 Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: Used to display advertisements
  • Google Analytics: Used for website analysis (if applicable)
  • Other Analytics Tools: Used to improve service quality

5. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your information security:

  • Encrypted Transmission: Use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Access Control: Limit access permissions to personal information
  • Security Monitoring: Continuously monitor for abnormal activities and security threats
  • Data Minimization: Only collect information necessary to provide services
  • Regular Review: Regularly review and update security measures

6. Data Retention

Our data retention policy:

  • Analysis Data: Usually retained for 30 days for technical analysis
  • Access Logs: Retained for 90 days for security monitoring
  • Aggregate Statistics: Anonymous statistical data retained indefinitely
